Why should I accept the local currency?

To minimise cash out to local suppliers and purchase tools and services without spending cash, whilst increasing your customer base.


What about taxes?

Parity sits on your balance sheet like cash and all the usual taxes are due. We encourage sellers to charge VAT in pound sterling.


How do I use Parity?

It is available on mobile and web application. You can post listings on our marketplace and view what is available.


How can the credit be zero interest?

The zero interest credit is only available in the marketplace to spend with other members. It cannot be withdrawn.
