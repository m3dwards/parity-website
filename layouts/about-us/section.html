What Parity is all about

Parity means equal, and that’s what we’re here to do: to level the playing field for small businesses like us. Whether it’s the zero-interest credit in our local currency, access to our marketplace or our simple app that brings you to Parity, we’re here to put spending power into the hands of local business owners.

Local currency. Local spending. Local wins.

Our Values

Clear

We’ll keep it simple & transparent so you know what you’re getting, what we’re doing and why it matters.

Cooperative

We’re not here to disrupt the system, consider us part of the fabric help however we can our local community.

Considerate

We support local companies and operate a members first and Parity second mentality; we can’t exist without you.

Curious

We promise never to utter the words “that’s the way it’s always been done” or stop looking for better ways of doing what we do.
