How does it work for my business?

Parity is a Birmingham business network with the option to buy and sell without using pounds.

The local currency is additional liquidity and not a replacement for your existing finances.

It is simply another line on your business balance sheet and treated like any other asset.

What do we believe in?

Mutual benefit

Trade in Parity is a cooperation between buyers and sellers for collective gain.

Trusted connections

Long-lasting and fulfilling partnerships among members, with Parity as a guarantor.

Regional resilience

Local trade is efficient, environmentally friendly and strengthens communities with shared values.

Our Mission

Independent businesses will thrive in Parity.

We aim to facilitate millions of transactions among thousands of local companies.

We want to support inclusive growth in diverse communities across Birmingham.

Our Development

Parity is a result of research on local currencies at the University of Birmingham.

We have built our own technology tailored to the needs of our members.

We will continuously develop to meet the needs of the network.
